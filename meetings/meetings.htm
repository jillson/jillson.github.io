Annual Meeting

Carleton Shores Association Annual Meeting

The Annual Meeting of the Carleton Shores Association is held each year on the fourth Sunday in August. Due to the size of the membership, the meeting is held outdoors. Kathy Siranosian has been kind enough to host the meeting at her home at 25 Weathervane Lane. This year's meeting will be held at 4:00 p.m. on August 26th, 2018. A confirmation of location will be sent along with the notice of the meeting.

The purpose of the meeting is to conduct the business of the Association, elect new officers to the Board, discuss and vote on the annual budget, and to review the past year's work of the Carleton Shores board of directors.

A mailing containing more information about the meeting will be sent to all members on or about August 13th.
