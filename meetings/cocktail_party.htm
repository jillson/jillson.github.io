Carleton Shores Annual Cocktail Party

Each year in July, the neighborhood gets together for an afternoon (adults only) cocktail party. The party is a great way for neighbors to make connections with each other, make new friends, and have a good time.

The 2017 annual cocktail party was hosted by Amy and Jim Rogers at their house on the afternoon of July 15th. The 2018 party is still TBD

If you are interested in hosting next year's event, please contact Mike Myers or any of the board members.
