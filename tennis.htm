Carleton Shores Tennis Club

Membership to the Carleton Shores Tennis Club is separate from Carleton Shores Association membership. The Club is open to all residents of Carleton Shores and use of the tennis courts is restricted to Tennis Club members and their family and guests. We hope you are a tennis player yourself, as we’re always looking for new folks to play with, but many Carleton Shores residents have joined the Tennis Club either as a show of support for the neighborhood or to ensure access to the courts for their guests.

We have 2 private tennis courts, located on the right just off of Lost Meadow Road, which were resurfaced 5 years ago and are in excellent condition. The annual membership dues are currently set at $150 per year per household. This amount is revisited annually based upon costs of property taxes, insurance, court repairs, etc. Since the courts were resurfaced fairly recently we do not anticipate any major expenditures for maintenance.

If you do decide to become a member of the Tennis Club, please just complete the enclosed tennis application and return as directed.

We hope you will consider joining us. If you have any questions or comments, or if you’d just like to get acquainted, please feel free to contact us.
