Beach Clean-up Days

Each year, on the first or second Saturday in June, volunteers gather at the beach for the purpose of cleaning up the beach for the start of the summer season. If you are new to the neighborhood, this is a great way to connect with your neighbors, contribute to the beauty of our beach, and makes new friends.

For 2017, the first beach clean up will be on June 3rd at 12pm; the second beach cleanup is July 5th, again starting at 12pm (This is the Saturday after July 4th, to clean up any fireworks mess.)
