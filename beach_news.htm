Beach Clean-up Day
Each year, on the first Saturday in June, volunteers gather at the beach for the purpose of cleaning up the beach for the start of the summer season. If you are new to the neighborhood, this is a great way to connect with your neighbors, contribute to the beauty of our beach, and makes new friends.

Date: Saturday, June 2nd
Time: 9:00 a.m.
Place: meet at beach parking lot
Rain date: Sunday, June 3rd

Please bring your own gloves and a plastic trash bag.
